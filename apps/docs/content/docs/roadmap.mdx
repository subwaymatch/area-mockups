---
title: Roadmap
description: Where the library is heading next.
---

- **2D mockups**: CSS/SVG renderers sharing the same device dimensions
  (exported today as `PHONE`, `GALAXY_VARIANTS`, and friends) and the same
  content API, for zero-WebGL contexts like screenshots and emails.
- More devices: TVs, foldables, more monitors and wearables.
- Deep-link camera poses and scroll-driven animation helpers.
