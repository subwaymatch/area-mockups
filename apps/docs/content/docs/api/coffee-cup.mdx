---
title: Coffee cup
description: A paper hot cup whose curved kraft-sleeve wrap is live DOM.
---

`<CoffeeCup>` and `<CoffeeCupMockup>` render a disposable hot cup — tapered
body with a rolled lip, sip lid, and a kraft sleeve — where the sleeve wrap
is the library's first **curved** live surface. You supply ONE artwork laid
out at the full unrolled wrap size; the cup tiles it around the curve from
16 flat strips, each clipping its own slice, and each strip hides itself as
it turns away from the camera. Spin the cup and the wrap reads continuous,
seam and all.

<ObjectDemo demo="cup-basic" />

```tsx
<CoffeeCupMockup autoRotate>
  <YourSleeveWrap />
</CoffeeCupMockup>
```

## Props

Beyond the [shared screen props](/docs/screen-content):

| Prop | Type | Default | Description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `variant` | `'12oz' \| '16oz'` | `'12oz'` | Cup size. Both share lip/base diameters; the 16 oz is taller. |
| `lid` | `boolean` | `true` | Show the sip lid. Turn off to reveal the coffee. |
| `color` | `string` | `'#f4f3ef'` | Paper cup color. |
| `sleeveColor` | `string` | `'#c9a97c'` | Kraft sleeve — also shows through transparent wrap areas. |
| `lidColor` | `string` | `'#f6f5f2'` | Lid plastic. Try `'#2b2b2e'` for black. |
| `wrapBackground` | `string` | `'transparent'` | CSS background behind the wrap. |
| `resolution` | `number` | `1024` | CSS px width of the FULL unrolled wrap. |
| `interactive` | `boolean` | `false` | See the caveat below. |

**The curved-surface caveat:** the wrap artwork is duplicated into every
strip, so stateful or interactive content will not stay in sync across the
curve — curved surfaces want artwork, not apps. That's also why
`interactive` defaults off here. Size your art against
`COFFEE_CUP_WRAP_ASPECT` (height ≈ 0.22 × the unrolled width).
