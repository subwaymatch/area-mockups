`<Fold>` renders a Galaxy Z Fold 7; `<FoldMockup>` is the all-in-one version.
One device, two form factors: **open** (default) fills your content onto the
large, nearly square inner display — a faint crease runs down its center — and
**closed** (`open={false}`) folds to the tall cover display with the rear
triple camera on the back.

<ObjectDemo demo="fold-open" checker />

```tsx
<FoldMockup color="#c9ccce" frameColor="#b9bcbe">
  <YourApp /> {/* fills the big inner display */}
</FoldMockup>
```

## Props

Beyond the [shared screen props](/docs/screen-content) and, on the mockup, the
[shared mockup props](/docs/api):

| Prop | Type | Default | Description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `open` | `boolean` | `true` | Unfolded tablet (content on the big inner display, with a center crease) versus the folded candy-bar (content on the tall cover display, rear triple camera showing). |
| `orientation` | `'portrait' \| 'landscape'` | `'portrait'` | Landscape lays the device on its side and swaps the virtual display with upright content. |
| `color` | `string` | `'#3a3d42'` | Back panel colorway. |
| `frameColor` | `string` | `'#54585f'` | Metal frame, buttons, hinge, and camera rings. |
| `punchHole` | `boolean` | `true` | Front camera punch hole overlay (top-right on the inner display, centered on the cover). |
| `resolution` | `number` | per state | CSS pixel width of the active display; height follows the panel aspect. Defaults to the inner display when open (820×911) and the cover display when closed (360×816). |

## The two states

The active display is whichever screen is showing, so your content maps onto
the inner display when open and the cover display when closed. Switch with a
single prop:

<ObjectDemo demo="fold-closed" />

```tsx
<FoldMockup open={false} autoRotate float color="#3a3d42" frameColor="#54585f" screenBackground="#000">
  <LockScreen /> {/* now on the tall cover screen */}
</FoldMockup>
```

## Colorways

Try Silver Shadow `#c9ccce` with `frameColor="#b9bcbe"`, Blue Shadow `#4a5568`,
or Jet Black `#26282c` with `frameColor="#3a3d42"`.
